# CRO TESTING FRAMEWORK (8-Step Cycle)

**Systematic hypothesis-driven optimization:**

1. **Identify Goals** - Immediate (clicks, ATC), Campaign (purchases, ROAS), Long-term (LTV, retention)
2. **Gather Data** - Google Analytics, heatmaps (Hotjar), customer feedback
3. **Analyze Data** - Find conversion rate, drop-off points, friction
4. **Develop Hypotheses** - Format: "We believe [CHANGE] for [AUDIENCE] will make [OUTCOME] happen"
   - Example: "Adding customer photo reviews for first-time visitors will increase ATC 15%"
5. **Design Variants** - One variable per test, prioritize high impact + low effort first
6. **Implement Tool** - Google Optimize/VWO, define conversion event, 50/50 traffic split
7. **Run Test** - Until 95% statistical significance (see duration chart below)
8. **Analyze Results** - Did hypothesis prove true? Document learnings, decide next test

**Test Duration (to 95% confidence):**

| Daily Conversions | 2 Variants | 3 Variants |
|-------------------|------------|------------|
| 10/day            | 16 days    | 23 days    |
| 25/day            | 7 days     | 10 days    |
| 50/day            | 4 days     | 6 days     |

**Impact:** 3-5% weekly compound improvement = 20-30% total lift over 6 weeks
