## 5. CIALDINI'S 6 PERSUASION PRINCIPLES

Integrate these psychological triggers into all touchpoints:

1. **RECIPROCITY** - Give value first
   - Free duvet buying guide before asking for sale
   - Welcome email series provides education before offer
   - Apply to: Lead magnets, welcome series, blog content

2. **COMMITMENT & CONSISTENCY** - Start small
   - Email signup before purchase ask
   - Quiz creates micro-commitments
   - Apply to: Cart abandonment ("You were so close!"), progress bars

3. **SOCIAL PROOF** - Others are doing it
   - "2,847 Canadians sleep better with EcoComfort"
   - Customer photos, reviews, testimonials
   - Apply to: All landing pages, product pages, ads

4. **AUTHORITY** - Trust the expert
   - OEKO-TEX certification prominently displayed
   - "Made in Canada" authority signal
   - Apply to: Product descriptions, trust pages, ads

5. **LIKING** - Be relatable
   - Authentic founder story
   - Friendly, helpful tone ("We get it...")
   - Apply to: Email voice, brand story, customer service

6. **SCARCITY** - Limited availability
   - "Order by Dec 20 for Christmas delivery"
   - Countdown timers, stock counters
   - Apply to: All sales periods, shipping deadlines
