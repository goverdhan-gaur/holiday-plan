### 7. CRO Testing Framework (8-Step Cycle)

1. **Identify Goals** ‚Üí What are you optimizing for?
2. **Gather Data** ‚Üí Analytics, heatmaps, behavior
3. **Analyze Data** ‚Üí What's hurting conversion?
4. **Develop Hypotheses** ‚Üí "We believe [change] will cause [outcome]"
5. **Design Variants** ‚Üí Create test versions
6. **Implement Technology** ‚Üí Set up testing tools
7. **Run Test** ‚Üí Until statistical significance
8. **Analyze Results** ‚Üí Why did it work/fail?

**Testing Roadmap:**
- Week 1: Landing page headline (3 variants)
- Week 2: Ad creative (lifestyle vs product)
- Week 3: Email subject lines (urgency vs curiosity)
- Week 4: Discount format (% vs $ vs free gift)
- Week 5: Checkout flow (one-page vs multi-step)
- Week 6: Retargeting message (reminder vs new offer)

**Impact:** 3-5% weekly compound improvement = 20-30% total lift
