# ECM-58: ABO (Ad Set Budget Optimization) Strategy Guide

**Task Type:** Alternative Strategy Guide
**Use When:** You want manual control over budget distribution

---

## Overview

Complete alternative to CBO strategy with manual budget control at ad set level.

## CBO vs ABO Summary

**CBO (Campaign Budget Optimization):**
- Budget at CAMPAIGN level
- Meta decides distribution
- Best for: Scaling proven campaigns, budgets $500+/day
- Less work, faster optimization

**ABO (Ad Set Budget Optimization):**
- Budget at AD SET level  
- YOU decide distribution
- Best for: Testing phase, budgets $100-300/day, learning
- More control, easier diagnosis
- **← RECOMMENDED FOR WEEK 1**

## Campaign Structure (ABO)

**Campaign:** Holiday 2024 - Cold Traffic - ABO (NO campaign budget)
- Ad Set 1: Broad Targeting - $70/day
- Ad Set 2: Natural Living - $40/day
- Ad Set 3: Sleep & Wellness - $40/day
- **Total: $150/day**

## Daily Optimization

**Day 1-3:** DO NOT TOUCH (learning phase)
**Day 4-7:** Optimize based on ROAS:
- >4.0x → Increase budget 50%
- 3.0-4.0x → Increase 25%
- 2.0-3.0x → Keep same
- 1.5-2.0x → Decrease 25%
- <1.5x → Pause
